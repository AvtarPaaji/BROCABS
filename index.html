Brocabs FAQs

How to book the Brocab service?

You can easily book Bro Cab services by either filling out the form on the website or by calling us at 9810514484, 9810509835.

Can I book a cab/taxi from and for Delhi Airport?

Yes, you can easily book a cab or a taxi for and from the Delhi Airport for on-time pick-up and drop services. To do this you must fill out the form on the website or call us at 9810514484, 9810509835 at least 2-3 hours in advance to get a confirmed booking.

Can I book a taxi during lockdown in Delhi? Is it safe?

You can book Brocab's safe and sanitized taxi service even during the lockdown period. The cabs are disinfected and thoroughly cleaned before & after every ride and contain a bottle of sanitizer for customer's use. Brocab's drivers are trained to take necessary precautionary measures as per the government guidelines to ensure your safety on roads..

Are Brocabs any cheaper than other cab/taxi providers?

Brocabs believe in fair pricing even during surge times. With us you will always get a reliable cab/taxi service at a much cheaper price than other providers.

Would I get a refund if I cancelled a service?

In case you cancelled a service you are entitled for a refund (T&Cs applicable). You must call us at 9810514484 or 9810509835 to settle any refund related issues.

Are the vehicles insured and drivers verified at Brocabs?

We value fair business practices and customer's safety. Hence, all our cabs are fully inspected, insured and their respective drivers verified before they can join our team. We conduct a thorough background check of all our drivers and maintain an updated KYC all the time.
